 Before and After Care Tips : 
 To ensure you get the best results from your beauty enhancement treatments , follow these helpful tips : 
 Before Your Appointment : 
 Arrive with clean skin ( no makeup or lotions ) 
 Avoid using an eyelash curler or applying mascara before an eyelash lift 
 For spray tans , exfoliate and moisturize your skin the day before 
 After Your Appointment : 
 Avoid getting your lashes or brows wet for the first 24 hours 
 Refrain from rubbing or touching your treated areas 
 After a spray tan , avoid sweating or getting wet for the first few hours to ensure the tan sets properly

 Frequently Asked Questions : 
 How long do the results of an eyelash lift last ? An eyelash lift typically lasts between 6 and 8 weeks , depending on your natural lash growth cycle . After this period , your lashes will gradually return to their natural shape , at which point you can schedule another appointment for a touch - up . 
 Is eyebrow lamination safe ? Yes , eyebrow lamination is completely safe when performed by a trained professional . The treatment involves applying a gentle solution to set your brow hairs in place , giving them a polished look . There are no harsh chemicals involved , and the procedure is non - invasive . 
 Can I go swimming after a spray tan ? It ’ s recommended to avoid swimming , heavy sweating , or any activities that involve water for at least 4 - 6 hours after your spray tan . This allows the solution to set properly and ensures longer - lasting results . 
 How soon can I see results from these treatments ? Results from an eyelash lift or eyebrow lamination are typically visible immediately after the procedure . A spray tan provides instant results , giving you a radiant , bronzed look right away .
